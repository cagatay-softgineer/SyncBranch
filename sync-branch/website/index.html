Welcome to Yggbranch.dev

Introduction

SyncBranch is a cutting-edge mobile application designed to help you explore and discover your unique music identity. By integrating with your Spotify account, SyncBranch analyzes your listening habits, uncovering patterns in the music you love. Through this analysis, SyncBranch connects you with others who share your musical tastes and personalities.

Our Mission

At SyncBranch, we believe that music is more than just sound—it's a reflection of who we are. Our mission is to create a community where music enthusiasts can connect based on their shared love for certain rhythms, moods, and genres. Inspired by the ancient myth of Yggdrasil, SyncBranch uses the power of music to build connections between people, much like the World Tree connects the nine realms of Norse mythology.

Technologies Used

Spotify API

We integrate with the Spotify API to access real-time user data, analyze track features such as danceability, energy, and mood, and personalize music experiences based on user history.

Flutter

Our mobile application is built using Flutter, a UI toolkit for crafting high-performance, visually stunning apps for both iOS and Android platforms from a single codebase.

Python

Our backend services are powered by Python, handling data analysis, API communication, and server-side logic to provide real-time responses to the mobile application.
